Music Programs

Little Flowers offers individual instruction in voice, piano, guitar, and music theory. Our curriculum engages students to ensure both learning and enjoyment.

Our program is aligned with international standards of music education. Individual instruction is at the heart of our Music Program and is available to children from beginners to advanced students.

Our professional faculty members are dedicated to making each child's experience stimulating and fun. Our private lessons ensure that your child receives personalized attention and expert guidance as they develop their musicality.

The Voice Program

Our primary emphasis is on the mastery of singing technique. We follow the "essential singing" technique and show our students to become confident and expressive vocal performers.

Students will learn proper breathing, tone production, diction, and performance skills through a variety of musical styles.

The Piano Program

Our piano program provides a strong foundation in piano skills and a pathway for progressive study in piano.

Each session is carefully structured to include technical exercises, theory building, sight-reading, and repertoire development.

Guitar Program

Our guitar program offers comprehensive training in both classical and contemporary guitar techniques.

Students will learn proper hand positioning, chord progressions, fingerstyle techniques, and music theory as applied to the guitar.

Music Theory

Our music theory program provides the foundation for understanding the language of music.

Students will learn notation, rhythm, harmony, ear training, and composition skills that will enhance their performance abilities.
