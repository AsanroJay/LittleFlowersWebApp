Dance Programs

Little Flowers offers a variety of engaging dance classes designed to nurture creativity, discipline, and self-expression in students of all ages.

Whether your child is taking their first steps in dance or building on years of experience, our dance programs provide a safe and inspiring space to grow. Classes are led by experienced professionals who foster a love for movement and the performing arts.

Students learn technical skills, choreography, and performance etiquette in a nurturing and encouraging environment tailored for both recreational and aspiring pre-professional dancers.

Musical Theatre Program

For our students who prefer 3-in-1 classes that combine singing, acting, and dancing. A dynamic and expressive program ideal for those who love the stage.

The Piano Program

For students aged 15 and up who enjoy learning ballet with peers in a private, in-studio setting. Focused on both technique and enjoyment.

Guitar Program

Designed for ages 3–5 (Baby Ballet) and 6+ (Pre-Ballet), these classes encourage early movement, rhythm, and creative exploration in a joyful and structured setting.
